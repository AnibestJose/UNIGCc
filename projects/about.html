ABOUT

Over the past 50 years, Universal Global College has transformed itself from a local community college to a worldly institution, with a presence in countries like NIgeria, Ethiopia, Egypt and South Africa. No matter who you are, or where you’re from, your journey to success can start here.

MISSION

UNIGC is a vital resource for building and sustaining community, standing ready to serve in times of emergency, airing locally relevant information and entertainment to listeners, creating a forum through which every voice can be heard. Our station offers instruction and experience in radio production and broadcasting skills to members of all ages.

VALUE
